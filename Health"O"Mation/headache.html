Headache

What Is It?

A headache is a pain in your head that can make you feel dizzy. It can be described as a pressure that is throbbing, constant, sharp or dull. It is a very common illness that occurs numerous times to one in their life.

Sustainable Cures

1. Headaches can be caused because of lack of sleep. Make sure you rest frequently and take naps during the day

2. It can also be caused due to lack of water and dehydration. So, make sure you drink sufficient amount of water everyday to reduce the pain.

3. Headaches are also caused due to posture. Don’t be in positions like half-sleeping and half- sitting. When you are sitting, sit upright and when you are sleeping, lay down properly.

4. People also get headaches because they skip their meals. This is not a good idea, because you need nutrients to function your body, especially if you are growing up or are in teenage years. Make sure you never skip your meals and eat regularly, and at the right amount

5. Rest a wet cloth on your forehead. Do this while laying down and closing your eyes

Non-Sustainable Cures

1. Pain relievers like acetaminophen usually help. However, go to a doctor before using and don’t use too much. This may cause rebound headaches.

2. Aspirin is also another way to prevent headaches
