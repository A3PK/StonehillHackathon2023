Skin Infection

What Is It?

Skin infections occur when bacteria infect the skin and sometimes the deep tissue beneath the skin. This causes pimples, rashes, redness, pain, pus and itching. This infection is common worldwide.

Sustainable Cures

1. Apply cold compresses to your skin several times a day to reduce itching and inflammation.

2. Use topical creams and ointment to reduce itching and discomfort.

3. Frequent hand washing is one of the best ways to reduce skin infections

4. Garlic. Cultures across the world have long recognized garlic for its preventive and curative powers

5. Honey. Since the time of Aristotle, honey has been used as an ointment that helps wounds to heal and prevents or draws out infection

Non-Sustainable Cures

1. Clindamycin is a good medicine to treat skin infections

2. Trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole is also a good medicine
