Abdominal Pain

What Is It?

Abdominal pain is a discomfort anywhere in your belly region between your ribs and pelvis. Abdominal pain is mistaken as stomach ache but it does not come from your stomach. It comes from your abdomen. This is a common illness as many usually get it once in a while for some or the other reason. All ages can be bound to get this.

Sustainable Cures

1. Drinking water helps reduce this discomfort and pain in your abdomen. Being dehydrated makes digestion more difficult and less effective

2. Avoid spicy food: avoiding this also helps cure this illness

3. Avoid lying down. When the body is horizontal, the acid in the stomach is more likely to travel backward and move upward, which can cause heartburn.

4. Eating ginger also helps reduce abdominal pain

5. Avoid eating difficult to digest foods like: - Fatty or acidic foods - Wheat products - Fruit and fruit juices such as watermelon - Spicy foods - Greasy foods

Non-Sustainable Cures

1. Aluminium Hydroxide can help relieve this pain

2. Magnesium hydroxide also helps relive abdominal pain
