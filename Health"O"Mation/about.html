About Us

There will no longer be bills for you

We are students of Grade Seven coming from Bangalore International School. Our group name is Health”O”Mation. Hi, our names are Ayan Das, Aadhvik Sudhakaran and Arnav Saraogi. We have joined the Stonehill Hackathon 2023-24 in order to create an awareness of being sustainable and related to the topic of good health and wellbeing. Adding to being sustainable, it also saves your money and helps the poor. We created a website using HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript. Our websites include common illness and their cures. Or we should say, sustainable cures. These sustainable cures teach you how to be sustainable without the need of ordering medicines. Since medicines are expensive, it costs a lot of money, giving you a sickening bill. However, if you follow our sustainable cures, these are money free and just requires attention. Now, viola! No more money comes out of your pocket and you are also eco-friendly to the environment. In addition to money, when poor people get sick, they cannot afford proper medicines. But if they follow these sustainable cures, they will also recover! Our website features a campaign on how to be more sustainable in daily life and be more environmentally friendly to the environment. That is not it, though! Our website comes with a set of questions to test your knowledge about what you learned. So what are you waiting for? Got sick? Follow our 100% correct home remedies to cure your illness.
