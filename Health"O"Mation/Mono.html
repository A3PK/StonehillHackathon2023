Mononucleosis

What Is It?

Mononucleosis is a contagious infection caused by a herpes virus called Epstein-Barr. Other viruses can also cause mono. This is common for teenagers and young adults. People with this face extreme fatigue, fever and body aches. This can be a sore throat and swollen glands. Here are sustainable ways to cure this infection. Symptoms will most probably improve after 2 weeks.

Sustainable Cures

1. It can be caused by sharing glasses and spoons. So, try to reduce sharing the same

2. Take a lot of rest. You may feel so tired that you will not get out of bed for at least a week

3. Drink a lot of fluids. Drinking water and other liquids help reduce this infection

4. Avoid contact sports for 2 months after you get the infection

5. Avoid weightlifting for 2 months after you get the infection

Non-Sustainable Cures

1. Pain relievers such as acetaminophen (Tylenol) help reduce this infection

2. Ibuprofen (Advil) can also reduce this infection along with acetaminophen.
