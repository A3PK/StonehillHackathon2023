Bronchitis

What Is It?

Bronchitis is a condition characterized by inflammation of the bronchial tubes, which are the air passages that lead to the lungs. It is usually caused by a viral or bacterial infection, and can also be triggered by environmental irritants such as tobacco smoke, air pollution, and dust.
