Contribute to our project

1. Click the button "contribute" to donate. You will be redirected to the donations page.


2. Register by inputting the information asked. Your personal information will be kept privately.


3. Donate 100 Rupees or more and upload a screenshot of your page as evidence.


4. Submit the form. You will get a certificate for your contribution that includes your name, our logo and signature.


5. A tree named after YOU will be planted and taken care of.
