Why forests are crucial for climate protection

Forests play a crucial role in mitigating climate change by absorbing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. However, deforestation and forest degradation are contributing to greenhouse gas emissions, making the protection and restoration of forests a priority in the fight against climate change.

Global CO2 emissions rose less than initially feared in 2022

New data from the International Energy Agency shows that global CO2 emissions rose less than expected in 2022, thanks to the growth of clean energy sources that offset the impact of greater coal and oil use.

Denmark to achieve almost zero from electricity and district heating

Denmark has announced its plans to achieve almost zero greenhouse gas emissions from electricity and district heating by 2030. The country is already a leader in renewable energy and aims to reduce its overall carbon emissions by 70 percent compared to 1990 levels by 2030. The plan includes a significant expansion of wind and solar power, as well as investments in energy-efficient buildings and industrial processes.
